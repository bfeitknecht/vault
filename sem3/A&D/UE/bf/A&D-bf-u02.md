## (a)
$\boxed{n^5+4n^4 \leq \mathcal O\left( \frac{n^6}{5}\right)}$

Thus, $f=n^5 + 4n^4$ and $g=\frac{n^6}{5}$. We apply the theorem and compute the limit.
$$
\begin{align}
\lim_{ n \to \infty } \frac{f(n)}{g(n)} &= \lim_{ n \to \infty } \frac{n^5+4n^4}{\frac{n^6}{5}}
\end{align}
$$
