## (a)
Let $f:\N \to \N$ denote the number of words of length $n$ over $\Sigma$ that contain each of the three letters at least once.

$$
f(n) \def \begin{cases}
0, & n < 3 \\
3^n - 3 \cdot 2^n - 3 \cdot 1^n, & n \geq 3
\end{cases}
$$
