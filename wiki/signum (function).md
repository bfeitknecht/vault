The **signum** [[function]] returns the *sign* of its argument, $\mathrm{sgn} : \mathbb{R} \to \{ -1, 0, 1 \}$. It is defined piecewise as below.
$$
\mathrm{sgn}(x) = \begin{cases}
-1, & x < 0 \\
0, & x = 0 \\
1, & x > 0
\end{cases}
$$
