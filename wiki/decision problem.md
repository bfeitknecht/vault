
$$
\begin{align}
&A:\Sigma_1^* \to \Sigma_2^*  \\
A \text{ solves decision problem } (\Sigma, L) &\iff \forall x \in \Sigma^*:A(x)=\begin{cases}
1, &x \in L \\
0, & x \not\in L
\end{cases}
\end{align}
$$
One can also say, $A$ recognizes the [[language]] $L$.

$(\Sigma, L)$ and $\exists A$ that recognizes $L$ $\implies$ $L$ is *recursive*.
