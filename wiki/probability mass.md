The **probability mass** of a discrete [[random variable]] $X$ is a [[function]] $p_{X} : \mathbb{R} \to [0, 1]$ that yields the probability that the variable is exactly equal to some value. It is defined as follows.
$$
p_{X}(x) = \Pr[X = x]
$$
