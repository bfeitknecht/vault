A **logical formula** $F$ in [[propositional logic]] is a [[arity|k-ary]] *[[function]]* from the [[set]] of truth assignments for its [[constants|proposition symbols]] $A, B, C, \dots$ to truth values, i.e. $F :\{0, 1\}^k \to \{0,1\}$.
