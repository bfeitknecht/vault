A **logical formula** in [[propositional logic]] is a [[arity|k-ary]] *[[function]]* from the [[set]] of truth assignments for its [[constants|proposition symbols]] $A, B, C, \dots$ to truth values, i.e. $\{0, 1\}^k$ to $\{0,1\}$.
