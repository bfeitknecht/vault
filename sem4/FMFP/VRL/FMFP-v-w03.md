# Notes
Lambda calculus function application is similar to haskell anonymous functions.
$$
\begin{align}
\lambda x.x 
&\leadsto (\lambda x.x)(y)  \\
&\leadsto (\lambda x.x)[x \to y]  \\
&\leadsto y
\end{align}
$$
